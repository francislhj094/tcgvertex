# 📈 Growth & Scaling Strategy

Strategic roadmap to grow PokéPrice Tracker from MVP to profitable business.

## Phase 1: MVP Launch (Month 0-1)
**Goal: 1,000 users, validate product-market fit**

### Week 1-2: Pre-Launch
- [ ] Soft launch to friends and Pokémon TCG communities
- [ ] Gather initial feedback and fix critical bugs
- [ ] Create social media accounts (Twitter, Instagram)
- [ ] Prepare launch announcement

### Week 3-4: Public Launch
- [ ] Post on Reddit: r/pkmntcg, r/PokemonTCG, r/SideProject
- [ ] Share on Twitter with #PokemonTCG hashtag
- [ ] Submit to Product Hunt
- [ ] Reach out to 5 Pokémon TCG YouTubers for review
- [ ] Post in Pokémon TCG Discord servers

### Success Metrics
- 1,000+ unique visitors
- 100+ cards added to watchlists
- 5-10% affiliate CTR
- Collect 50+ email addresses for updates

## Phase 2: Content & SEO (Month 2-3)
**Goal: 5,000 organic visitors/month via SEO**

### SEO Content Strategy
Write 2-3 blog posts per week targeting these keywords:

#### High-Intent Keywords (Buyers)
- "[Card Name] price" - Create individual pages for top 100 cards
- "most valuable pokemon cards 2026"
- "pokemon card investment guide"
- "how to track pokemon card prices"

#### Long-Tail Keywords
- "is [card name] worth buying right now"
- "pokemon card price tracker free"
- "best pokemon cards to invest in"
- "pokemon tcg price alerts"

### Content Ideas
1. **Weekly Market Report** - "Top 10 Price Movers This Week"
2. **Set Reviews** - "Scarlet & Violet Price Guide: What's Worth It?"
3. **Investment Guides** - "Pokémon Cards That Tripled in Value in 2025"
4. **How-To Guides** - "How to Use Price Alerts to Save Money"
5. **Card Spotlights** - Deep dives on high-value cards

### Technical SEO
- Add structured data (JSON-LD) to card pages
- Create XML sitemap with all card URLs
- Optimize meta descriptions for click-through rate
- Build internal linking between related cards
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console

### Link Building
- Guest post on Pokémon TCG blogs
- Get listed on "best Pokémon TCG tools" articles
- Partner with card shops for backlinks
- Create shareable infographics (most valuable cards)

### Success Metrics
- 5,000+ organic visitors/month
- 50+ keywords ranking in top 10
- 200+ indexed pages
- 10+ backlinks from relevant sites

## Phase 4: Multi-Platform (Month 7-12)
**Goal: 50,000 users across web and mobile**

### Mobile App (React Native)
Why build a mobile app:
- Push notifications for price alerts (higher engagement)
- Barcode scanner for adding cards
- App Store SEO (new discovery channel)
- Better user retention

#### Features
- Scan cards with camera (image recognition)
- Instant price lookup
- Price alert push notifications
- Offline mode (cached prices)
- Dark mode

#### Monetization
- Free tier with ads (AdMob)
- Premium subscription ($4.99/month, same as web)
- One-time purchase: $19.99 lifetime (alternative to subscription)

### Progressive Web App (PWA)
Convert website to PWA:
- Add to home screen
- Offline support
- Push notifications on web
- App-like experience

### Success Metrics
- 50,000 total users (web + mobile)
- 20,000 app installs
- 500 premium subscribers
- $2,500+ MRR

## Revenue Optimization

### Affiliate Income ($5K-15K/month at scale)
#### Increase CTR
- A/B test button copy ("Buy Now" vs "View Best Price")
- Show price comparison table (TCGPlayer vs eBay)
- Add "Lowest Price" badge on best deals
- Create "Best Place to Buy" recommendation

#### Increase Commission
- Negotiate higher rates with TCGPlayer (after 10K clicks/month)
- Add Amazon Associates (books, supplies)
- Partner directly with card shops (5-10% commission)

### AdSense Income ($2K-8K/month at scale)
#### Increase CPM
- Target high-value keywords (investment, grading, vintage)
- Create content about expensive cards ($1000+)
- Attract collector demographic (higher ad value)

#### Increase Impressions
- Add ad unit in watchlist page
- Place ad after every 4 cards in search results
- Add sticky footer ad on mobile (subtle)

### Premium Subscriptions ($10K-50K/month at scale)
#### Increase Conversions
- Show "Premium Only" badge on locked features
- Offer annual plan prominently (better LTV)
- Add referral program (1 month free per referral)
- Trial reminder email sequence (days 1, 4, 6)

#### Reduce Churn
- Send monthly value emails ("You saved $X with alerts")
- Add "pause subscription" instead of cancel
- Survey churned users for feedback
- Implement feature requests from premium users

### Fixed Costs
- **Hosting**: $20-50/month (Vercel Pro)
- **Domain**: $15/year

### Variable Costs
- **Pokémon TCG API**: Free tier → $10-50/month at scale
- **Email Sends**: $0.10 per 1000 emails
- **SMS Alerts**: $0.01 per message (optional premium feature)
- **Payment Processing**: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (Stripe)

### Total Year 1 Costs: $500-1,500
